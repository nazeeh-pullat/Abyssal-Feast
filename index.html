Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Abyssal Feast ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the game may collect limited non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device information, such as device type and OS version
  • Gameplay progress and in-game data, such as coins, unlocks, and progression
  • Anonymous usage data for improving the gameplay experience

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Save and load your game progress
  • Improve game performance and balance
  • Fix bugs and enhance the user experience

3. Third-Party Services

Abyssal Feast may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Analytics tools, if implemented
  • Advertising networks, if implemented

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing how they use such information.

4. Data Storage

Game data such as progress, coins, and unlocks may be stored locally on your device using technologies such as PlayerPrefs.

Some features, such as redeem codes, may fetch data from external sources, such as GitHub-hosted files, but do not collect personal user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, update, or delete. If you wish to reset your data, you can clear the app data through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
